About Bright Minds

Bright Minds is a development centre focused on playful and purposeful learning for children ages 4 to 8 in Calgary, Alberta. Our curriculum blends social-emotional learning, early literacy, numeracy, and hands-on exploration through games, art and movement.

Mission
To nurture confident, curious children ready for school and life.
Approach
Small groups, individualized attention, play-first activities.
